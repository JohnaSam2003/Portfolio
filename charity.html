The charitable organization My Friend's Place, which helps homeless youths transition into self-sufficiency, received $2.2 million in donations in all of 2018. This past weekend, in less than 24 hours, famously generous YouTuber Markiplier raised a whopping one-quarter of that in a livestream to benefit the charity. Markiplier, whose real name is Mark Fischbach, kicked off the weekend-long livestream on Feb. 23 on fundraising platform Tiltify. He hosted the first 24 hours into Sunday morning, and successfully rallied his following of 23 million YouTube subscribers to raise his goal of $500,000.
